Frequently Asked Questions

  • What type of Shoot Chandak Studio Provides?

    Chandak Studio provides Brand Shoot, Portfolio Shoots, Bridal Shoots, Advertisements Shoots, E-Commerce Products Shoots, Music Videos, You Tube Videos, and Chroma Shoots etc.

  • What kind of photography equipment do you use?

    I utilize a DSLR or mirrorless camera body paired with various lenses for diverse perspectives. A tripod stabilizes for long exposures. Lighting equipment like studio lights enhances control. Accessories include memory cards, bags, and filters. Editing is done with software like Adobe Lightroom for refining images.

  • Are you a successful photographer?

    Success in photography is subjective and varies for each individual. It depends on factors like artistic vision, technical skills, recognition, and personal fulfillment. I continuously strive to improve my craft, seeking opportunities for growth, and finding satisfaction in capturing moments that resonate with others.
